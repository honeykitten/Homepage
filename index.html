Education

My Education

The games I loved the most when I was a child were Simcity and Roller Coaster Tycoon. I felt satisfaction from creating my own world and developing it through feedback in the game. Thus, I became interested in the synergy between man and machine. I studied statistics and psychology in order to understand people better. I believe it is not enough to just propose something, but rather, it is necessary to hear direct feedback from clients in order to be satisfied with my work.

I ran a UX club with the help of my professor, and together we studied innovative UX techniques and methodologies. In addition, I entered many development competitions along with students from technologies and computer science faculties in a span of six months. Oct,2012 ~ Dec,2012

Smart Device UX/UI Design

This would allow a system in which the child, the guardian, and the doctor can all feel satisfied with the treatment process.
